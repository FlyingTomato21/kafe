Menu

In research & development

We're still in the kitchen. ka fe is in its research-and-development phase — sourcing beans and matcha, testing flavors, and dialing in every drink before we commit it to the menu.

Rather than post a menu we'll rewrite ten times before opening, we'd rather show you the real one when it's ready. What we can tell you now: it'll be expertly crafted coffee and matcha made with real care, a few signature drinks we've designed ourselves, and a small food menu — all in development for our Spring 2027 opening.

The espresso classics will all be there — cortados, cappuccinos, flat whites, lattes, americanos — made with great beans from great local roasters. And then there's the one that's already locked in.
