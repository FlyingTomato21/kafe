Our story

It started with making coffee every morning for someone. Exactly the way she likes it.

She can't have caffeine, so hers is always decaf. But she still loves coffee: the ritual, the warmth, the fact that someone made it just for her.

That's what ka fe is. Coffee made personal. We make our drinks here from scratch. The banana caramel, the mascarpone cream, the strawberry compote, the syrups. All in-house, by hand. And we pay the same attention to you as we do to the cup.

The name is simple. ka fe. Say it out loud and it sounds like "café." We liked that.

Come back twice and we'll know what you drink.
