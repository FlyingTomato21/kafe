The short version

It started with making coffee every morning for someone — exactly the way she likes it. That's what ka fe is: drinks designed with care, and the same attention paid to you. Coffee and matcha, made the way you take it. The name is the word for coffee — 咖啡 — and it sounds like "café." Both at once.
