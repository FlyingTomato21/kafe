## Priority Issues

### [P1] Form endpoint is placeholder
`YOUR_FORM_ID` will fail silently. Replace before deployment.

### [P1] No form submission feedback
`.signup-success` exists in CSS but no JS triggers it. Users see nothing after submit.

### [P2] Menu teaser leads with incompleteness
"The menu's still in development" creates emotional dip. Lead with the banana latte confirmation.

### [P2] Em-dash overuse
8 em-dashes is AI writing cadence. Vary punctuation.

### [P3] Numbered sections (01/02/03)
Risk of AI slop perception. Numbers add no semantic meaning.
