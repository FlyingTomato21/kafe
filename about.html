About

Ka Fe is a specialty coffee shop in Yonkers. We make creative lattes with real ingredients and pull shots with care.

Our signature drinks — ube cloud, pandan, caramelized banana — are made from scratch with ingredients we actually like. No artificial flavors, no neon syrups. Just good stuff in a cup.

The space is industrial. Exposed ducts, steel counters, concrete floors. It's not precious. We wanted somewhere that felt honest — a place to sit, think, work, or just drink something good without anyone hovering.

We care about the coffee. We source carefully, pull shots with attention, and keep things simple. If you want a specialty coffee shop that doesn't take itself too seriously, you're in the right place.

Yonkers is home. This is a neighborhood spot that happens to care about what's in the cup.
